Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our jewelry, classes, workshops, and more.

Do I need prior experience to take a class or workshop?

No prior experience is necessary for our beginner classes and most workshops. We provide all the instruction, tools, and materials you need to create beautiful jewelry pieces. Our classes are designed to be accessible to complete beginners while still offering techniques that more experienced jewelry makers will enjoy.

What should I wear to a jewelry making class?

We recommend comfortable clothing that you don't mind getting a little dirty. Avoid loose sleeves or dangling jewelry that might get in the way while you're working. Closed-toe shoes are recommended for safety. We provide aprons for all participants.

Do I need to bring any tools or materials?

No, all tools and materials are provided for our classes and workshops. Everything you need to complete your projects is included in the class fee. If you have your own tools that you prefer to use, you're welcome to bring them, but it's not necessary.

What is your cancellation policy?

For classes and workshops, we require 48 hours notice for cancellations to receive a full refund. Cancellations with less than 48 hours notice will receive a credit toward a future class. For private parties, we require 7 days notice for cancellations to receive a full refund of your deposit.

How many people can attend a private jewelry making party?

Our private parties require a minimum of 6 participants. At our studio, we can accommodate up to 12 guests. For parties at your location, we can accommodate up to 20 participants, depending on the space available. Larger groups may require an additional instructor, which will affect pricing.

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes, we offer gift certificates for all of our classes and workshops. Gift certificates can be purchased for specific classes or for a dollar amount that the recipient can apply to the class of their choice. Gift certificates are valid for one year from the date of purchase.

Can I commission custom jewelry pieces?

Yes, Barbara creates custom jewelry pieces on commission. Please contact us with details about what you're looking for, and we'll schedule a consultation to discuss your ideas, timeline, and budget. Custom pieces typically require 3-4 weeks to complete, depending on complexity.

Are your classes suitable for children?

Most of our regular classes are designed for adults and teens 16 and older. However, we do offer special family workshops and children's classes for ages 10-15 during school breaks and summer. We also offer private parties specifically designed for younger participants. Please contact us for more information about options for children.

How do I care for my handmade jewelry?

Care instructions depend on the materials used in your jewelry. Generally, we recommend storing pieces separately to prevent scratching, removing jewelry before swimming or showering, and cleaning with a soft cloth. Specific care instructions are provided with each piece purchased or created in our classes.

Do you ship your jewelry?

Yes, we ship our jewelry throughout the United States and internationally. Domestic shipping is a flat rate of $8, and international shipping rates vary by location. All pieces are carefully packaged to ensure they arrive safely.

Still Have Questions?

If you don't see your question answered here, please don't hesitate to contact us. We're happy to help!

Workshop Information

What's Included

All materials and tools are provided for each workshop. You'll take home your completed jewelry pieces, plus detailed handouts to help you continue practicing at home.

Experience Level

Most workshops are suitable for all skill levels unless otherwise noted. Beginners are always welcome, and experienced jewelry makers will find new techniques to explore.

Registration

Pre-registration is required for all workshops. A 50% deposit secures your spot, with the balance due on the day of the workshop. Workshops often fill quickly, so early registration is recommended.

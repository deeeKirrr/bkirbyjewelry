Jewelry Making Parties

Get your friends together and host a jewelry making party in your home or business. Bring snacks or a bottle of wine!

This workshop is for anyone who wants to:

  • •Have a fun night out with friends
  • •Learn the basics of fine silver clay jewelry
  • •Create a silver pendant or a pair of earrings

This program is designed to give a brief overview of the silver metal clay process. $65 per person includes:

  • •2.5 to 3-hour "party style" workshop
  • •Post workshop firing and finishing, basic findings included
  • •Time permitting you & your guests will be able to make additional pieces, which can be paid for by weight

Unfired pieces will be taken for firing and finishing and will be returned to the hostess in 1-2 weeks.

Minimum 6 people. 6-8 is a great number!

For more information email barbara@bkirbyjewelry.com or call 914 629-3705 to book your party date.

Parties can be scheduled in advance most days or evenings.

** Hostess Perk ** 15 grams of silver clay

** Metal clay is provided by instructor at market price.

Jewelry Making Parties

Host a unique and memorable jewelry making party for your special occasion.

Host a Unique Party Experience

Looking for a creative and fun activity for your next special occasion? Host a jewelry making party with B Kirby Jewelry!

Whether it's a birthday celebration, bridal shower, corporate team building, or just a fun gathering with friends, our jewelry making parties offer a unique and memorable experience for everyone.

Each guest will create their own beautiful piece of jewelry to take home as a lasting memento of your special event.

Party Options

Bracelet Party

Guests will create beautiful custom bracelets using a variety of beads, charms, and findings.

Duration: 2 hours

Price: Starting at $45 per person

Earring Party

Each guest will design and create 1-2 pairs of earrings with professional guidance.

Duration: 2 hours

Price: Starting at $40 per person

Necklace Party

Create stunning pendant necklaces with a variety of materials and techniques.

Duration: 2.5 hours

Price: Starting at $50 per person

Party Details

Location Options:

  • At our studio in San Francisco (up to 12 guests)
  • At your location (within 25 miles of San Francisco)

What's Included:

  • All tools and materials needed for the projects
  • Professional instruction and assistance
  • Gift packaging for completed pieces
  • Setup and cleanup

Minimum Guests: 6 people

Booking: 2-week advance booking required with 50% deposit

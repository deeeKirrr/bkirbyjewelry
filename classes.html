Class Information

What to Expect

All classes are held in our well-equipped studio in San Francisco. Class sizes are kept small (maximum 8 participants) to ensure personalized attention and guidance.

No prior experience is necessary for beginner classes. All tools and materials are provided, and you'll take home your finished pieces at the end of each class.

Advanced classes build on basic skills and are perfect for those looking to expand their jewelry making repertoire.

Booking Information

Advance registration is required for all classes. A 50% deposit is needed to secure your spot, with the balance due on the day of the class.

For private group bookings or custom class arrangements, please contact us directly.

About Barbara Kirby

Discover the story behind B Kirby Jewelry and the passion that drives our creations.

My Journey

Barbara Kirby is a skilled jewelry artist with over 20 years of experience in crafting unique, handmade pieces. Her passion for jewelry making began in her early twenties and has evolved into a lifelong dedication to the craft.

Barbara specializes in combining traditional techniques with contemporary designs, creating pieces that are both timeless and modern. Her workshops are designed to share her knowledge and inspire creativity in others.

After studying metalsmithing and jewelry design at the California College of Arts, Barbara honed her skills working with master craftspeople before establishing her own studio in San Francisco.

My Philosophy

Quality Materials

I believe in using only the highest quality materials in my jewelry, ensuring each piece is not only beautiful but durable and long-lasting.

Sustainable Practices

Environmental responsibility is important to me. I source materials ethically and implement sustainable practices in my studio.

Sharing Knowledge

Teaching others the art of jewelry making is my way of preserving traditional techniques and inspiring the next generation of artisans.
